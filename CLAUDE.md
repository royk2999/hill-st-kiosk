# Project Instructions

## Figma → HTML conversion

When converting Figma designs to HTML, reproduce the design **pixel-perfect** — a 1:1 match of the Figma file. Do not add anything, change anything, or make suggestions unless explicitly asked.

**Standing exception:** The red `<marquee>` banner (`background: #fe4531`) at the top of `index.html` is intentional and should be kept.
